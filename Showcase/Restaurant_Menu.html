Menu


1- Pepperoni Pizza


Enjoy our delicious pepperoni pizza, a perfect combination of flavors. This pizza is made with a thin, crispy dough base,
topped with a rich tomato sauce and a generous layer of melted mozzarella cheese. The succulent and spicy pieces of pepperoni
are distributed evenly, providing a touch of irresistible flavor in every bite. Ideal to share with friends or enjoy on a movie night
with our delivery service. Enjoy it from $8.99 depending on your chosen size.


2- Pizza Carbonara


Delight yourself with our exquisite carbonara pizza, a fusion of flavors that will transport you to Italy. This pizza starts
with an artisanal dough base, baked to the perfect balance of crispy and soft. Covered with a creamy white sauce that combines
Parmesan cheese and egg, this delicacy is garnished with pieces of crispy bacon and freshly ground black pepper, creating a contrast
of flavors and textures that you will love. Every bite is a unique culinary experience. Don't miss it! Here or at home with our delivery
service. Enjoy it from $8.99 depending on your chosen size.


3- Red and Green peppers Pizza


Discover the freshness and vitality of our red and green pepper pizza. This delicious creation begins with a base of artisanal dough,
crispy and golden, perfect to enhance each flavor. The pizza is generously topped with a rich tomato sauce and a rich blanket of melted
mozzarella cheese. Vibrant red and green peppers add a pop of color and a sweet, fresh flavor, while aromatic herbs like oregano and basil
elevate the experience to the next level. Each bite is an explosion of flavor and a feast for the senses. Ideal for veggie lovers and those
looking for a light but satisfying option! Enjoy it here or in the comfort of your own home with our delivery service, from $9.99 depending
on your chosen size.


4- Make your own pizza


Unleash your creativity with our Make Your Own Pizza option! Now you are the chef. Start by choosing your favorite base,
whether it's a classic, whole wheat or gluten-free dough. Then, select from a wide variety of fresh, flavorful ingredients: from classics
like pepperoni, ham, and cheese, to vegetarian options like spinach, olives, and an explosion of colorful peppers. Don't forget to add a
special touch with our exclusive sauces and fresh herbs. Each pizza is unique, just like your palate! Design a combination that reflects your
personal style and enjoy a culinary experience that only you can create. *The price may vary depending on your chosen size and ingredients*
